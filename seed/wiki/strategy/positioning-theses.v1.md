Claude Code power-users have three acute pains: (P1) session amnesia, (P2) bloated-context cost, (P3) no file search over their documents. Each is promotable with single-pillar hooks per §1.1 of the design spec.

## Hypothesis T2
Hosted CTA wins when the pain does not require BYO storage; OSS CTA wins when it does. Mis-routed videos produce low signup conversion.
